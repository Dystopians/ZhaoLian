# 35 — Project Glossary

## Product terms

- **案卷 / dossier:** the in-game collection of evidence, claims, timeline, sources, and glossary.
- **主张 / claim:** a statement about history whose support and conflicts are explicitly modeled.
- **证据卡 / evidence card:** a player-acquired record of an observed, heard, or documented detail with provenance.
- **材料层级 / source class:** documented, testimony, later research, composite, reconstruction, or unresolved.
- **叙事封闭 / closure:** hidden tendency to complete uncertain records into precise sentences; not a morality score.
- **证据克制 / rigor:** hidden tendency to preserve source limits and blanks.
- **见证关怀 / care:** hidden tendency to preserve daily and relational detail and avoid coercive questioning.
- **门槛规则 / threshold rule:** once Zhao leaves the house, the camera and player cannot follow him.
- **当前轮证据 / current-run evidence:** evidence actually acquired in the active run.
- **跨轮知识 / meta archive:** material seen in previous completed runs, not admissible as current-run observation.

## Historical names

- **郁达夫 / Yu Dafu:** modern Chinese writer and anti-Japanese cultural worker; true/public name relevant to literary and political history.
- **赵廉 / Zhao Lian:** alias/social identity used in West Sumatra.
- **赵老板 / Zhao Boss:** local social address used in the game's reconstructed town perspective.
- **赵胡子:** attested/reported local nickname; use only after source review and explanation.
- **巴爷公务 / Payakumbuh:** city in West Sumatra; historical Chinese transliteration used in sources.
- **武吉丁宜 / Bukittinggi:** nearby West Sumatran city associated with interpreter work in accounts.
- **胡愈之 / Hu Yuzhi:** writer/editor and key early investigator of the disappearance.
- **创造社:** modern Chinese literary society co-founded by Yu Dafu; appears in appendix biography, not as main gameplay content.

## Source codes

- **D:** documented/strongly established.
- **T:** testimony or recollection.
- **L:** later research reconstruction.
- **C:** composite material.
- **R:** dramatic reconstruction.
- **U:** unresolved.

## Engineering terms

- **ink:** narrative scripting language used for story flow.
- **inkjs:** JavaScript runtime/implementation used to run compiled ink in the browser.
- **golden path:** deterministic test route to a required ending/state.
- **historical lint:** automated checks against missing source metadata and prohibited certainty.
- **save migration:** conversion from an older save schema to a current one.
