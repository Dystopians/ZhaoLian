# 00 — Executive Summary

## Product

**Title:** 《出去一趟：赵廉案卷》  
**Format:** browser-based historical interactive narrative  
**Primary language:** Simplified Chinese  
**First playthrough:** 75–100 minutes  
**Completionist play:** 2–3 hours  
**Business model:** free, no ads, no monetization  
**Deployment:** static hosting

## Player fantasy

The player is an unnamed contemporary archival editor/translator. They cannot alter the past. They choose what to observe, how to translate, how to question witnesses, how to grade evidence, and what wording enters a public record.

## Core loop

1. Read a reconstructed scene.
2. Choose an observation, translation, or question.
3. Receive a claim/evidence card with a source class.
4. Compare conflicting records in a dossier.
5. Draft a final record whose certainty reflects the player's methods.

## Fixed historical outcome

- Zhao Lian leaves home on the night of 29 August 1945 after a young man calls at the door.
- He does not return.
- The player cannot prevent, follow, reverse, or explicitly witness this event.
- The strongest overall attribution points to Japanese military police, while exact date, method, location, and remains remain uncertain in the game.

## Differentiators

- Choices alter historiography, not history.
- Translation is a moral and mechanical system, not a vocabulary quiz.
- The most important interaction after the departure is a repeated **Wait** action.
- No true ending; four final texts expose different editorial values.
- Historical source layers are visible and testable.

## Success criteria

The product succeeds when players:

1. Understand that Zhao Lian and Yu Dafu are one person in different social worlds.
2. Understand that evidence, testimony, inference, and reconstruction are not interchangeable.
3. Feel the pressure of waiting without being offered a false rescue path.
4. Recognize Japanese military-police responsibility without mistaking uncertain details for settled fact.
5. Finish with awareness that archival language itself exercises power.
