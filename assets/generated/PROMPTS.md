# Generated Media Prompt Record

Date: 2026-06-19.

Mode: built-in `image_gen`, followed by local Pillow resize/compression to WebP.

Shared visual constraints for all generated images:

- original low-detail silhouette or cut-paper illustration;
- warm paper, ink charcoal, humid green-gray, muted amber, and occasional dark-red route accents;
- no readable text, logos, watermarks, flags, emblems, gore, execution, corpse, grave, or fake documentary photograph;
- people shown as silhouettes, partial figures, backs, hands, or non-photographic portraits;
- West Sumatra represented as an inhabited civic world, not exotic scenery.
